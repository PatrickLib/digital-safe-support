Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data really stored offline?
Yes! DigitalSafe stores all your data locally on your device using Core Data. Your passwords and sensitive information never leave your device unless you enable iCloud sync, which uses end-to-end encryption.
What happens if I forget my master password?
If you've set up a recovery key or security questions, you can use them to reset your password. We recommend storing your recovery key in a safe place. Without these recovery options, there's no way to recover your data due to our zero-knowledge architecture.
How does iCloud sync work?
When enabled, your encrypted data syncs across all your Apple devices via iCloud. The data is encrypted before leaving your device and can only be decrypted with your master password. Apple cannot read your data.
How much does DigitalSafe cost?
DigitalSafe is a one-time purchase with no subscriptions or hidden fees. Pay once and enjoy all features forever, including all future updates.
How do I export my data?
Go to Settings > Export Data. You can export your data in encrypted format for backup purposes. We recommend keeping backups in a secure location.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Our Commitment

DigitalSafe is built with privacy as a core principle. We believe your personal data belongs to you and only you.

Data Collection

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Any of your stored passwords or sensitive data

Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using Apple's Core Data framework with encryption. If you enable iCloud sync, your data is encrypted end-to-end before being stored in your personal iCloud account.

Third-Party Services

DigitalSafe does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Zero-Knowledge Architecture

We use a zero-knowledge architecture, which means we have no ability to access, read, or decrypt your data. Your master password never leaves your device.
